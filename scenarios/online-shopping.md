# Scenario: Online Shopping Quality And Refund Disputes

Use this file for e-commerce purchases involving poor quality, fake goods, damaged goods, missing accessories, wrong items, or refusal of return/refund.

## Typical Fact Patterns

- The received goods are damaged, defective, counterfeit, expired, or materially inconsistent with the listing.
- The merchant refuses return or exchange by using store rules or "special-price no return".
- The platform delays or closes the case without addressing the evidence.

## Core Evidence

- Order details and payment record
- Product listing screenshots
- Unboxing video if available
- Photos and close-up videos of the defect
- Courier tracking information
- Chat records with merchant and platform

## Best Legal Framing

Usually combine:

- Goods not conforming to quality or agreed description
- Misleading advertising if the listing materially differs
- Unfair standard terms if the merchant blocks lawful return or exchange

## Merchant Message Template

```text
我于[日期]购买[商品名称]，订单号[XXX]，支付[XXX]元。收到货后发现[问题描述]，与页面描述/基本质量要求不符。我已保留商品照片、视频和订单记录。

请按法定或约定售后义务，在[24/48]小时内为我办理退款、退货或换货，并明确后续流程。
```

## 12315 Complaint Template

```text
投诉人于[日期]在[平台/店铺名称]购买[商品名称]，订单号[XXX]，金额[XXX]元。收货后发现商品存在[问题]，与页面描述或质量要求不符。

投诉人已于[日期]联系商家/平台要求[退款/退货/换货]，但对方以[拒绝理由]拒绝处理或拖延处理。

投诉人请求：
1. 责令被投诉人依法办理[退款/退货/换货]；
2. 如核查认定存在虚假宣传或欺诈，请依法处理；
3. 督促被投诉人就本次消费纠纷作出明确答复。

证据：订单截图、商品页面截图、实物照片/视频、聊天记录、物流记录等。
```

## Practical Advice

- Lead with the clearest mismatch: size, material, function, authenticity, damage, or safety issue.
- If there is an unboxing video, mention it early.
- If the goods are potentially fake or unsafe, do not let the merchant rush you into deleting evidence first.

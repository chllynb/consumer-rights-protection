# Scenario: Food Safety And Restaurant Disputes

Use this file for spoiled food, foreign objects, expired food, unhygienic conditions, or illness after dining.

## Typical Fact Patterns

- Foreign object in food
- Food spoilage or obvious contamination
- Expired or improperly stored food
- Physical discomfort after eating, especially with receipts and medical records

## Core Evidence

- Receipt, order record, or payment proof
- Photos and video of the food and packaging
- Store name, address, and dining time
- Preserved food sample if possible
- Medical records, invoices, and diagnosis if illness occurred
- Chat and call records with the merchant

## Best Legal Framing

Usually combine:

- Food safety risk
- Goods or services not meeting safety standards
- Duty to handle consumer complaints promptly

## Merchant Message Template

```text
我于[日期时间]在[门店名称]消费，订单/小票金额[XXX]元。食物中出现[异物/变质/其他问题]，相关照片、视频及消费凭证已保存。

请贵店立即说明情况并提出处理方案。如涉及食品安全问题，我将同步向监管部门投诉并保留进一步追责权利。
```

## 12315 Complaint Template

```text
投诉人于[日期时间]在[门店名称/平台]购买或食用[食品名称]，支付[XXX]元。消费过程中发现[异物/变质/过期/食后不适]等问题。

投诉人已保存消费凭证、现场照片/视频、剩余食品及相关就医材料，并已于[日期]联系商家，但对方[拒绝处理/仅愿意私下小额补偿/拖延]。

诉求：
1. 依法处理本次消费纠纷并给予退款、赔偿或其他合理补救；
2. 对涉事食品及门店经营行为进行核查；
3. 将处理结果反馈投诉人。
```

## Practical Advice

- If the user became ill, tell them to preserve the full medical chain.
- If the user still has the food item, advise not to discard it too early.
- Be careful with compensation language unless the facts strongly support a food-safety standard violation.

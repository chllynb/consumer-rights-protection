# Scenario: Prepaid Card, Membership, Training, Beauty, And Gym Disputes

Use this file when a merchant takes prepaid funds and then closes, relocates, shrinks services, refuses refund, or becomes unreachable.

## Typical Fact Patterns

- The store closes suddenly after collecting prepaid fees
- The merchant forces transfer to another distant branch
- The available services are materially reduced
- The merchant cites a no-refund clause in the membership agreement
- Multiple consumers are affected at the same time

## Core Evidence

- Contract, sign-up form, receipts, and payment records
- Membership balance screenshots
- Merchant notices, closure photos, or relocation notices
- Chat records and call logs
- Advertisements promising long-term service or branch availability
- Other victims' public posts if available

## Best Legal Framing

Usually combine:

- Failure to continue performance
- Unfair standard terms
- Misleading sales statements
- Possible fraud indicators if the merchant took funds while unable or unwilling to perform

## Merchant Message Template

```text
我于[日期]在[商家名称]购买[会员/课程/储值卡]，支付[XXX]元，目前剩余权益/余额为[XXX]。现商家出现[闭店/停业/迁店/服务缩水/无法预约]，导致合同目的难以实现。

请贵方在[24/48]小时内提供书面处理方案，包括退款金额、退款时间和联系人；如逾期不处理，我将向12315、12345等渠道投诉，并保留进一步追责权利。
```

## 12315 / 12345 Complaint Template

```text
投诉人于[日期]在[商家名称]购买[会员/课程/储值服务]，支付[XXX]元，剩余未消费金额或次数为[XXX]。目前商家出现[闭店/停业/失联/服务无法履行]，已严重影响消费者权益。

投诉人已于[日期]联系商家要求退款或继续履约，但对方[失联/拒绝退款/仅以格式条款拒绝]。

诉求：
1. 督促被投诉人退还剩余未消费金额[XXX]元；
2. 核查其预付式消费经营行为是否存在违法违规问题；
3. 如涉及多人受损，请协调相关部门跟进处理并反馈结果。
```

## Practical Advice

- If many consumers are affected, say that clearly in the complaint.
- Preserve screenshots before the merchant deletes mini-program pages or group chats.
- If there are strong fraud signs, advise the user to consider police consultation in parallel.
